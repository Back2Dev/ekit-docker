The Key Personnel Questionnaire (Q10) should be given to people inside your organization (people who report to you, colleagues, key support personnel, etc.).

NOTE: Please do not give one of these forms to your supervising manager or partner as he/she will receive separate, more specialized questionnaires from MAP.

If you would like for us to contact them and send them the form for you, we will be happy to do so.  If so, please provide their names and email addresses below, and we will send them an email, giving them the opportunity to complete the form online.

And once again, please make sure to encourage those internal sources to complete and submit the form no later than <%duedate%>.

Before distributing these questionnaires, please check with your manager or partner, if possible, to be sure on who should receive them.
