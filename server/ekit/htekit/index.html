Pre-Workshop Kit Login Page  
Participants at MAP workshops are required to complete a number of short forms which provide vital information about their management abilities.

We also gather information from the participant's supervising manager and peers on their perceptions of the participant's management abilities. These forms are part of the "Pre-Workshop Kit (PWI)" and are completed in advance so that the workshop leader has a dossier of information on each participant before they arrive to the workshop itself. MAP's consultants tailor the workshop to the needs of the participants, allowing them to adapt it to their specific needs in continuing to improve their management skills.
